# Recipe for cinnamon s cookies

## Ingredients

- 200 g butter
- 2 dl sugar
- 2 egg
- 5 dl wheat flours
- 2 tsp baking powder

### Cinnamon-sugar on top
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 3 tbsp sugar

## Instructions
1. Soften (butter) in the microwave or at room temperature. Add the sugar. Beat until the mixture becomes pale.
2. Add the eggs one at a time, beating until light and fluffy.
3. Add the flour–baking powder mixture through a sieve. Mix until smooth.
4. Cover the dough and place it in the refrigerator. Let the dough firm up for some time.
5. Roll the dough into a thin log (Ø 1 cm). Use a little flour if needed. Cut the log into 8 cm pieces.
6. Mix sugar and cinnamon in a shallow container, for example a baking dish. Roll the logs in the mixture. Transfer them to a baking tray lined with baking paper. Shape them into an S shape.
7. Bake in the middle/upper part of the oven at 200°C for about 10 minutes, or until done and nicely browned.
